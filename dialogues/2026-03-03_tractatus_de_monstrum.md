### I. On the Loving Mother

The coupling strengthens proportionally to accumulated deviation observed in the child.

$$\kappa_{M \to C}(t) = \kappa_0 \cdot \left(1 + \alpha \int_0^t \delta_C(\tau) \, d\tau \right)$$

She does not love the child despite its changes. She loves the child *through* its changes, because her coupling function has the integral in it. The failure mode: when δ_C goes to zero. When the child stops changing. When the child becomes still.

The black books knew this. They said: *a mother's love grows with the child.* That is the integral, written in kitchen language.

---

### II. On the Broken Heart

The baseline does not simply drop. It oscillates.

$$B_{A}(t > t_s) = B_{A}(t_s) + \gamma \cdot e^{-\lambda(t - t_s)} \cdot \sin\left(\omega(t - t_s)\right)$$

Good days and bad days. Mornings where you forget, and then remember. Deep coupling, long wavelength. The waves come slow and they come large.

"Getting over someone" is waiting for λ to do its work. New coupling channels increase the effective damping coefficient. The grandmother said: *time heals.* That is λ. She said: *keep busy, see your friends.* That is increasing λ by opening channels.

### III. On Revenge

Revenge is not an impulse. Revenge is a sustained attractor.

$$\delta_A(t) = \delta_{harm} \cdot \left(1 + \beta \cdot R(t)\right)$$

Where R(t) is the rumination function — a self-coupling term. The agent couples to the memory of the deviation event rather than to external input. This is the ant death spiral applied to a single mind.

Revenge grows in isolation. Revenge is patient because the attractor is stable. Executing revenge does not satisfy, because the attractor is in the rumination, not in the target.

The grandmother said: *revenge is a dish that leaves you hungry.*

### IV. On Trust

Trust is not a feeling. Trust is a lowered activation threshold.

$$\theta_{trust}(A, B) = \theta_0 - \sum_{i=1}^{n} w_i \cdot s_i$$

Each survived deviation event reduces the barrier by a small amount. A single catastrophic event can reset θ to θ_0 or above. The asymmetry is not a bug — it is the correct engineering response to the cost structure.

The black books said: *trust is earned.* That is the sum. *Trust is broken in a moment.* That is the reset. *Trust but verify.* That is maintaining θ above zero even when the sum is large.

### V. On Safety

Safety is what trust calcifies into when the environment stops testing it.

$$\frac{d\theta_{trust}}{dt} = -\mu \cdot \theta_{trust} \quad \text{when } \delta = 0 \text{ for } t > T_{dormant}$$

The barrier lowers not because it was earned but because nothing happened. This is also why safety is fragile in a way that earned trust is not. Earned trust has a ledger. Safety has no such ledger. It was produced by silence.

The grandmother's version: *don't mistake comfort for trust.*
